Knowledge is potential — not results

Why smart people stay broke: power isn’t what you know. It’s what you do with it.

Why smart people stay broke: power isn’t what you know. It’s what you do with it.

Knowledge is potential, not results. You can know nutrition and still live on takeout. You can know how to sell and never dial a prospect.

The brain loves swapping action for studying — productive procrastination. Another book, another course: progress feelings, zero outcome.

Cheat code

Don’t wait for the perfect moment. Your brain will never say “ready now.” The gap between knowing and money is action.

Anti-course week

This week: zero new courses. One action from what you already know. Small. Finished. Visible results beat perfect plans.
