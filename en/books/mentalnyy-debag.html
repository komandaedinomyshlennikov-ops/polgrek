Mental Debug Reboot your brain in five minutes a day

If you’re asking “how do I force myself to start?” — forcing yourself means fighting your own brain. Motivation isn’t the cause of action. It’s a byproduct. Move first. The brain sees action and adapts. Not the other way around.

Ten minutes of start beats an hour of waiting for mood. Debug the loop: where did action stop? Short physiology-first resets when motivation books make you angry.
