Wired for Wealth How your brain shapes money — and what to do about it

“Money isn’t math. It’s neurobiology.”

Knowledge is potential, not results. The brain is great at swapping action for studying: another book, another course — progress vibes, zero outcome. Power isn’t what you know. It’s what you ship.

Dopamine is anticipation, not happiness. Loss hurts more than gain feels good. Stress tunnels the brain into now. Choice architecture beats willpower speeches: friction on impulse, ease on saving.
