FAQ

Is this just another biohacking grift?

No “become superhuman” course, no miracle stack, no secret club. In the books, key claims use evidence grades A–D where it fits, plus clear “what doesn’t work.” Free excerpts first; buy on LitRes or Amazon — not a paywall here.

Why write in Russian if he was born in England?

His wife, Laura Grek, is a clinical psychologist (Moscow State University). Pol started Russian at 35 for family and shared language. The Russian books and Threads are for that audience. This English site is the US-facing edition of the same work.

Who owns the science voice?

Pol writes applied neuropsychology as popular science: A–D labels where claims warrant them — not “everything is proven.” Laura co-authors selected titles (anxiety, hormones, EI) from clinical practice. Not a treatment plan — health decisions stay with your clinician.

Where do I buy — and how do I know it’s real?

Check LitRes (litres.ru/author/pol-grek/) and Amazon product pages linked from each book. This site is a showcase, not a store: checkout happens on those platforms. Social is optional proof, not the main paper trail.
