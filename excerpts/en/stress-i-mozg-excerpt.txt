Stress and the Brain
Stop burning out — start using stress as fuel

The brain loves the feeling of control. Even when it doesn’t have any. Especially then. Panic in uncertainty isn’t weakness — it’s a system without a clear next step. Give it one concrete move and the volume drops.

The amygdala does not negotiate with affirmations. Give the system one concrete next step. Breath, sleep, boundaries, and a stress profile beat “just relax.” Chronic load is a design problem.
